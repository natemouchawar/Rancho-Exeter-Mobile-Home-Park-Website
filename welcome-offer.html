Our way of saying we're glad you're here.

For New Residents

A House-Warming
From Us

Moving into a new community is a meaningful step, and we want yours to feel easy from the very first day. Anyone who moves a new mobile home into Rancho Exeter receives three months of free lot rent.

Consider it our way of welcoming you. Use those months to settle in, meet your neighbors, and let Rancho Exeter start to feel like home — without the rent on your mind.

A Welcome Worth Sharing

For Residents Who
Refer a Neighbor

The best parts of life here are the people, and the strongest communities grow when residents bring along the friends and family they love most.

Current residents who refer a new neighbor — someone who moves a home into Rancho Exeter — receive three months of free lot rent as our thank-you. The welcome you give a new neighbor is worth as much as the one we extend ourselves.

Reach Out

Have Questions?

Our office is happy to walk through the details — eligibility, timing, and what it looks like to bring your home or refer a neighbor. We'd love to hear from you.
